Weapon Description - "The Walking Stick"

The Walking stick is for all the sneaky old people who want to keep separated from others. When people see you with your walking stick they will think you’re an easy target and will try to attack you. It’s simple, if you see a threat you press the trigger and the rest is history. The walking stick will cost 20 chickens or 10 chickens and 2 buckets of water.
