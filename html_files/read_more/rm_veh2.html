Vehicle Description - "The Hamster Ball"

The Hamster ball is created by a someone who well took advantage of the world going insane. He was able to create something so dumb and unrealistic that can only exist in a game. he created a bulletproof hamster ball that shoots out red electricity that can apparently nuke a place? It doesn’t make sense but the person has gotten bored of this invention and has decided to sell it for a farm (if you own a farm and is thinking about purchasing this invention do be aware that if you try to kill him after the purchase the vehicle can only work if he’s alive).
