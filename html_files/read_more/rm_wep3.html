Weapon Description - "The Skunk potion"

There are 2 different ways to use this gem of an item, you throw it or you drink it. If you want to keep separated from people for the rest of your life drink it. You may have a burned throat, but you will have a smell so unbearable people within a 3-mile radius may vomit themselves to death. This potion was originally meant to be a health potion but due to a complete failure of "a little tweak of chemistry" this science experiment instead made the most effective way to murder while hating yourself. This is free we no longer want it.
